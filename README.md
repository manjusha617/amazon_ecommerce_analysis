# 🛒 Amazon E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard

A comprehensive Data Analytics and Statistical Analysis project designed to analyze Amazon product listings, pricing strategies, customer ratings, discounts, and category performance.

The project combines Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), Statistical Testing, Business Insights, and an Interactive Streamlit Dashboard to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Amazon hosts thousands of products across multiple categories. Understanding the factors that influence product performance, customer satisfaction, pricing effectiveness, and category growth is critical for sellers and businesses.

This project aims to analyze product data and identify the factors that influence:

* Product Ratings
* Customer Engagement
* Pricing Strategies
* Discount Effectiveness
* Category Performance

The findings help provide actionable recommendations for improving product visibility, customer satisfaction, and business growth.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze product pricing and discount patterns.
* Evaluate customer ratings and review behavior.
* Identify high-performing product categories.
* Investigate the relationship between price, ratings, and discounts.
* Apply statistical techniques to validate business assumptions.
* Generate business insights and recommendations.
* Build an interactive analytics dashboard.

---

## 📊 Business Questions

* Which product categories dominate Amazon listings?
* Which categories receive the highest ratings?
* How do product prices vary across categories?
* Do discounts influence customer ratings?
* Which products have the highest customer engagement?
* What factors affect customer satisfaction?
* What relationships exist between price, ratings, and discounts?
* Which categories provide the best business opportunities?

## 💡 Key Insights

* Product ratings are a strong indicator of customer satisfaction.
* Categories differ significantly in pricing, ratings, and discounts.
* High discounts do not always result in better customer ratings.
* Products with larger review counts generally gain greater customer trust.
* Certain categories dominate Amazon listings and customer engagement.
* Price alone does not guarantee better ratings or product popularity.
* Customer reviews strongly influence purchasing decisions.
